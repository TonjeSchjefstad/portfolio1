HEJ YOU!

I'm 33 and currently studying Frontend Development at Noroff. After spending 12 years at IKEA (the last 9 in leadership roles), I decided it was time for something completely different – and honestly, I'm having the time of my life learning to code!

These days I'm juggling studies with part-time work at a yarn store, which keeps me grounded while my brain wraps around HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. There's something oddly satisfying about both debugging code and untangling yarn – maybe it's the problem-solving aspect that appeals to me.

When I'm not staring at my laptop, you'll find me yelling at the TV during Formula 1 weekends (go Max!), working on my latest knitting project, or being completely owned by my two Siberian cats. My partner has learned to accept that race weekends are sacred, and that yes, I really do need another yarn purchase.

I love clean, minimalist design – probably comes from all those years surrounded by Swedish furniture – and I'm excited to bring my mix of leadership experience and fresh coding enthusiasm to creating websites that actually make sense to use.
